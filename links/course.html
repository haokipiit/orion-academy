JEE MAINS & ADVANCED

The Join Entrance Examination (JEE) is a two stage examination that consist of both JEE Mains and JEE Advanced. To get admission in various engieering colleges across the country like NITs and other engineering colleges, you need to clear JEE Mains and to get admission in the top tier colleges, i.e IITs the most prestigious colleges/university in the country, you need to clear JEE Advanced. To be able to appear for JEE Advanced, you are required to passed the JEE Mains.

Why apply for JEE?

To get admission in the most reputed engineering colleges in India and get a secure future by becoming an engineer or various further studies, it is require to appear both JEE Mains and JEE Advanced. The Orion Academy provides teaching and education based on the level of JEE Mains and Advanced. To get a proper guidance and knowledge, apply get register and get admission in The Orion Academy to receive the best quality education for JEE Mains and Advanced.

NEET - UG

The National Eligibility Entrance Test (NEET), formerly the All India Pre-Medical Test (AIPMT), is the qualifying test for MBBS and BDS programmes in Indian medical and dental colleges. It is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). A candidate who wishes to study medical courses needs to have Physics, Chemistry, Biology / Biotechnology as their compulsory subjects in Class 10th and 12th and must have passed these subjects.

Why apply for NEET?

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Examination is the only medical entrance examination in India to fill all the medical seats in India which includes the admissions to AIIMS and JIPMER, and also to the admissions of medical courses abroad. Qualifying NEET with high scores and secure a good rank in the All India Merit List enables the students to get a seat into the most prestigious medical college in the country.
